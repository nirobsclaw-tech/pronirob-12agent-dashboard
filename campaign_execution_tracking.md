## ðŸ“ˆ Lead Scoring & Qualification System

### Automatic Lead Scoring Matrix

#### Tier 1 Prospects (Score: 8-10)
**Qualification Criteria:**
- [ ] Monthly ad spend: $2,000+
- [ ] Decision maker confirmed (Marketing Manager, Founder, CMO)
- [ ] Specific tracking pain mentioned
- [ ] Active in last 7 days (ads, website updates, social)
- [ ] Response to initial outreach within 24 hours
- [ ] Budget authority confirmed or strongly implied

**Action Priority:** Immediate outreach, same-day response
**Expected Close Rate:** 40-60%

#### Tier 2 Prospects (Score: 5-7)
**Qualification Criteria:**
- [ ] Monthly ad spend: $500-$2,000
- [ ] Decision maker or strong influencer
- [ ] General performance concerns (could be tracking)
- [ ] Some activity in last 30 days
- [ ] Responds to outreach within 48 hours
- [ ] Budget available but needs justification

**Action Priority:** 24-hour follow-up, educational content
**Expected Close Rate:** 20-35%

#### Tier 3 Prospects (Score: 1-4)
**Qualification Criteria:**
- [ ] Monthly ad spend: Under $500
- [ ] Non-decision maker contact
- [ ] Growth stage but no specific tracking issues
- [ ] Limited recent activity
- [ ] Slow response time (48+ hours)
- [ ] Budget or timeline unclear

**Action Priority:** Nurture with educational content
**Expected Close Rate:** 5-15%

### Automation Workflows

#### Email Sequence Automation
```
TRIGGER: New prospect added to list
DAY 1: Send Email 1 (Problem Identification)
DAY 4: If no reply, send Email 2 (Case Study)
DAY 8: If no reply, send Email 3 (Social Proof)
DAY 11: If no reply, send Email 4 (Value Stack)
DAY 14: If no reply, send Email 5 (Final Follow-up)
DAY 21: Move to nurture sequence
```

#### Lead Response Automation
```
TRIGGER: Prospect replies to any email
IMMEDIATE: Send auto-reply with scheduling link
5 MINUTES: Notify via WhatsApp for manual follow-up
1 HOUR: If no manual response, send WhatsApp follow-up
24 HOURS: If no meeting scheduled, escalate to phone call
```

#### Meeting Follow-up Automation
```
TRIGGER: Discovery call completed
IMMEDIATE: Send thank you email + next steps
1 HOUR: Send proposal/invoice if ready
24 HOURS: WhatsApp follow-up for questions
48 HOURS: Final follow-up before moving to nurture
```
