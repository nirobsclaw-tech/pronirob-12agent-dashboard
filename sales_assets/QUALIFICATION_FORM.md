## Qualifying Questions (Phone/WhatsApp Call)

### Discovery Questions (Use if form unclear)
1. "Tell me about your current ad spend and what you're trying to track?"
2. "What specifically led you to believe there's a tracking issue?"
3. "Have you seen your conversion rates change recently?"
4. "What tools are you currently using for your analytics?"

### Urgency Assessment
1. "How much ad spend are you running with incomplete tracking?"
2. "What's your plan if this isn't fixed within [timeframe]?"
3. "Have you missed optimization opportunities because of tracking gaps?"

### Budget Qualification
1. "What would it be worth to you to get reliable tracking back?"
2. "How much are you losing in ad spend due to poor attribution?"
3. "Would you prefer to pay for fixes now or pay more later when issues compound?"

### Access Confirmation
1. "Are you ready to provide the access we need to fix this?"
2. "Is there anyone else involved in approving technical changes?"
3. "What's your timeline for getting access to these accounts?"

## Red Flags (Don't Pursue)
- Budget under $100 but ad spend over $1,000/month
- No admin access to any ad/analytics accounts
- Unrealistic expectations about timeline
- Only wants audit, refuses implementation
- Won't provide contact information
- Asking for work before payment (unless enterprise)

## Green Lights (High Priority)
- Ad spend $1,500+ with tracking issues
- Clear tracking problems they've noticed
- Urgency expressed
- Budget aligns with tier pricing
- Has admin access ready
- Open to video/phone consultation
