## ðŸŽ¯ Lead Scoring System

### Demographic Scoring:
- **Store Revenue**: $100k+ = 10 points, $500k+ = 20 points, $1M+ = 30 points
- **Industry**: Ecommerce = 15 points, B2B = 10 points
- **Location**: US/Canada = 10 points, UK = 8 points, Other = 5 points

### Behavioral Scoring:
- **Website Visits**: 5+ pages = 10 points
- **Time on Site**: 5+ minutes = 15 points
- **Resource Downloads**: 1-3 resources = 20 points, 4+ resources = 30 points
- **Email Engagement**: 70%+ open rate = 15 points, 25%+ click rate = 20 points

### Engagement Scoring:
- **Strategy Call Booked**: 50 points
- **Email Reply**: 25 points
- **Phone Call**: 35 points
- **Sales Page Visit**: 20 points

### Lead Qualification:
- **Hot Lead**: 70+ points = Priority follow-up within 2 hours
- **Warm Lead**: 50-69 points = Follow-up within 24 hours
- **Cool Lead**: 30-49 points = Continue nurturing
- **Unqualified**: <30 points = Long-term nurture sequence
