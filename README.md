# Pro Nirob - 12-Agent Team Dashboard

A comprehensive dashboard showcasing Pro Nirob's 12-agent business system for digital services and AI automation.

## Features

- **12 Specialized Agents**: Each agent represents a different aspect of the business
- **Real-time Status Monitoring**: Live updates on agent performance and status
- **Mobile Responsive**: Works seamlessly on all devices
- **Modern UI**: Clean, professional design with smooth animations

## Dashboard Agents

1. **Analytics Agent** - Google Analytics & Performance Tracking
2. **Automation Agent** - n8n Workflow Automation
3. **Content Agent** - Copywriting & Content Creation
4. **SEO Agent** - Search Engine Optimization
5. **Development Agent** - Custom Development & Solutions
6. **Marketing Agent** - Digital Marketing & Campaigns
7. **Analytics Agent** - Business Intelligence & Insights
8. **Communication Agent** - Client Relations & Support
9. **Design Agent** - Visual Design & Branding
10. **Strategy Agent** - Business Planning & Growth
11. **Technology Agent** - Tech Infrastructure & Support
12. **Quality Agent** - Quality Assurance & Testing
