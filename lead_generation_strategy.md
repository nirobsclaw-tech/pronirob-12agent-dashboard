## ðŸ“§ Cold Email Campaign Strategy

### Email Sequence Structure (5-email drip over 14 days)

#### Email 1: Problem Identification (Day 1)
**Subject:** "[Company Name] - Your [X]% revenue is invisible to Google Analytics"

**Body Structure:**
- **Hook:** Reference a specific metric/concern from their site review
- **Problem:** Explain how tracking gaps cause invisible revenue
- **Credibility:** Brief mention of recovery successes
- **CTA:** "Want to see exactly what you're missing?"

**Example Opening:**
```
Hi [Name],

I was reviewing [Company Name]'s Google Analytics setup and noticed something that might be costing you significant revenue. Your checkout process shows visitors completing purchases, but GA4 isn't properly tracking [specific metric].

This means Google and Facebook algorithms are working with incomplete data, potentially reducing your ad performance by 15-30%.
```

#### Email 2: Case Study/Proof (Day 4)
**Subject:** "How [Similar Company] recovered $47K in lost revenue"

**Structure:**
- Share anonymized case study of similar business
- Specific metrics of revenue recovery achieved
- Timeline of implementation and results
- Soft CTA for audit request

#### Email 3: Social Proof & Urgency (Day 8)
**Subject:** "Last chance - Google Analytics 4 changes impact your tracking"

**Structure:**
- Reference recent GA4 update affecting tracking
- Mention deadline/urgency (if applicable)
- Include testimonials/social proof
- Clear audit CTA

#### Email 4: Value Stacking (Day 11)
**Subject:** "What you're leaving on the table with incomplete tracking"

**Structure:**
- Break down specific revenue loss examples
- Compare against investment in tracking fix
- Risk of inaction messaging
- Final CTA with limited-time incentive

#### Email 5: Final Follow-up (Day 14)
**Subject:** "Quick question about [Company Name]'s tracking"

**Structure:**
- Brief, conversational tone
- Single focused question
- Easy out option
- Keep it short and respectful

### Email Personalization Framework

#### Level 1 (Basic Personalization):
- Company name, industry
- Personal name and title
- Recent company news/growth mention

#### Level 2 (Advanced Personalization):
- Specific tracking issues identified on their site
- Recent ad campaign references
- Competitor mentions or industry challenges
- Revenue size estimates based on traffic

#### Level 3 (Deep Personalization):
- LinkedIn profile insights
- Recent website changes/updates
- Team structure and decision-making process
- Specific pain points from reviews/forums
